# Sagittarius ♐ (November 22 – December 21)

**Element:** Fire  
**Modality:** Mutable  
**Ruling Planet:** Jupiter  

## Core Traits
Sagittarius is the explorer, philosopher, and truth-seeker of the zodiac. Sagittarius natives are adventurous, optimistic, and driven by a hunger for knowledge, travel, and expansive experiences. They embody freedom and higher learning.

## Strengths
- Adventurous and open-minded
- Optimistic and enthusiastic
- Philosophical and truth-seeking
- Generous and big-hearted
- Excellent sense of humor

## Challenges
- Restlessness and commitment avoidance
- Tactlessness and blunt speech
- Over-promising and under-delivering
- Difficulty with routine or detail work
- Can be preachy or self-righteous

## In Relationships
Sagittarius partners are fun-loving, honest, and generous. They need freedom and growth within relationships. They express love through shared adventures, humor, and philosophical conversations.

## Career Tendencies
Sagittarius excels in travel, higher education, publishing, law, philosophy, outdoor recreation, international business, and motivational speaking.

# Capricorn ♑ (December 22 – January 19)

**Element:** Earth  
**Modality:** Cardinal  
**Ruling Planet:** Saturn  

## Core Traits
Capricorn is the builder and strategist of the zodiac. Capricorn natives are ambitious, disciplined, and committed to achieving long-term goals through hard work and patient effort. They represent structure, authority, and mastery.

## Strengths
- Ambitious and goal-oriented
- Disciplined and responsible
- Patient and strategic
- Practical and resourceful
- Strong sense of duty and integrity

## Challenges
- Tendency toward workaholism
- Emotional reserve and difficulty being vulnerable
- Pessimism or excessive caution
- Can be controlling or rigid
- Difficulty relaxing or having fun

## In Relationships
Capricorn partners are loyal, dependable, and committed. They show love through providing stability, practical support, and long-term planning. They need a partner who respects their ambition and offers emotional warmth.

## Career Tendencies
Capricorn excels in management, finance, architecture, government, engineering, administration, and any field requiring strategic long-term planning.
