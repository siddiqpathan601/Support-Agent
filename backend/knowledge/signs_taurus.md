# Taurus ♉ (April 20 – May 20)

**Element:** Earth  
**Modality:** Fixed  
**Ruling Planet:** Venus  

## Core Traits
Taurus is the zodiac's stabilizer — grounded, sensual, and deeply connected to material comfort and beauty. Taurus natives value security, routine, and the finer things in life. They are patient builders who create lasting foundations.

## Strengths
- Reliable, dependable, and loyal
- Strong aesthetic sensibility and appreciation for beauty
- Patient and persistent
- Practical and financially savvy
- Deeply sensual and affectionate

## Challenges
- Stubbornness and resistance to change
- Possessiveness in relationships
- Can become overly attached to comfort and routine
- Tendency toward materialism or indulgence

## In Relationships
Taurus partners are devoted, sensual, and protective. They express love through physical affection, cooking, gift-giving, and creating a beautiful home environment. They seek stability and long-term commitment.

## Career Tendencies
Taurus thrives in finance, agriculture, real estate, culinary arts, music, fashion design, and any field combining beauty with practicality.
