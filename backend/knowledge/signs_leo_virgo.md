# Leo ♌ (July 23 – August 22)

**Element:** Fire  
**Modality:** Fixed  
**Ruling Planet:** Sun  

## Core Traits
Leo is the radiant performer and creative heart of the zodiac. Leo natives are generous, warm-hearted, and driven by a desire to shine and inspire. They have natural magnetism and a flair for dramatic self-expression.

## Strengths
- Natural charisma and leadership
- Generous and warm-hearted
- Creative and expressive
- Loyal and protective of loved ones
- Confident and courageous

## Challenges
- Need for attention and validation
- Pride and ego sensitivity
- Tendency toward drama or exaggeration
- Difficulty accepting criticism
- Can be domineering or controlling

## In Relationships
Leo partners are romantic, passionate, and generous. They love grand gestures, loyalty, and being adored. They express love through affection, gifts, and unwavering devotion. They need a partner who appreciates their warmth.

## Career Tendencies
Leo excels in entertainment, performing arts, leadership roles, politics, coaching, luxury goods, event planning, and any field allowing creative self-expression.

# Virgo ♍ (August 23 – September 22)

**Element:** Earth  
**Modality:** Mutable  
**Ruling Planet:** Mercury  

## Core Traits
Virgo is the analyst and healer of the zodiac. Virgo natives are detail-oriented, service-minded, and driven by a desire for improvement and practical perfection. They find meaning through being useful and refining systems.

## Strengths
- Exceptional attention to detail
- Analytical and methodical thinking
- Service-oriented and helpful
- Health-conscious and practical
- Humble and hardworking

## Challenges
- Perfectionism and self-criticism
- Tendency to worry and overthink
- Can be overly critical of others
- Difficulty relaxing or accepting imperfection
- May suppress emotions in favor of logic

## In Relationships
Virgo partners show love through acts of service, practical help, and thoughtful attention to detail. They may struggle with emotional vulnerability but are deeply devoted once committed. They seek a partner who appreciates their quiet devotion.

## Career Tendencies
Virgo excels in healthcare, editing, data analysis, research, nutrition, veterinary science, accounting, quality assurance, and any detail-oriented field.
