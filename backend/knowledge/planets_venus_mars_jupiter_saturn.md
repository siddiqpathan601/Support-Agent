# Venus ♀

**Rules:** Taurus and Libra  
**Exaltation:** Pisces  
**Detriment:** Aries and Scorpio  
**Fall:** Virgo  

## Significance
Venus represents love, beauty, pleasure, values, money, and relationships. It governs attraction, aesthetic preferences, how you give and receive love, and what you find beautiful. Venus shows your relationship style and what brings you joy and comfort.

## In the Birth Chart
Venus's sign shows your love language, aesthetic taste, and values. The house placement reveals where you seek harmony, beauty, and connection. Venus spends about 4-5 weeks in each sign but has a retrograde period every 18 months.

## Transits
Venus transits enhance social connections, romance, and creative expression. Venus retrograde (every 18 months, lasting 40 days) is a time for reassessing relationships, finances, and personal values rather than starting new romances.

# Mars ♂

**Rules:** Aries (and traditional co-ruler of Scorpio)  
**Exaltation:** Capricorn  
**Detriment:** Libra and Taurus  
**Fall:** Cancer  

## Significance
Mars represents action, desire, drive, aggression, energy, and sexuality. It governs how you assert yourself, pursue goals, handle anger, and express physical energy. Mars is the warrior planet — it shows how you fight, compete, and take initiative.

## In the Birth Chart
Mars's sign shows your action style, anger expression, and sexual energy. The house placement reveals where you direct your ambition and assertive energy. Mars spends about 6-7 weeks in each sign.

## Transits
Mars transits energize and activate the areas of life they touch. They can bring motivation and drive but also conflict and impatience. Mars retrograde (every 2 years) is a time for reassessing goals and redirecting energy rather than pushing forward aggressively.

# Jupiter ♃

**Rules:** Sagittarius (and traditional co-ruler of Pisces)  
**Exaltation:** Cancer  
**Detriment:** Gemini and Virgo  
**Fall:** Capricorn  

## Significance
Jupiter represents expansion, abundance, wisdom, higher learning, luck, and faith. It is the great benefic planet, bringing growth, opportunity, and generosity wherever it touches. Jupiter governs philosophy, religion, long-distance travel, and the search for meaning.

## In the Birth Chart
Jupiter's sign shows where you find faith, how you grow, and your philosophical outlook. The house placement reveals the area of life where you experience the most expansion and good fortune. Jupiter spends about 12 months in each sign.

## Transits
Jupiter transits bring opportunities, growth, and optimism to the areas they activate. Jupiter return (every 12 years) marks a new cycle of growth and purpose. Overindulgence and overconfidence are possible shadow effects.

# Saturn ♄

**Rules:** Capricorn (and traditional co-ruler of Aquarius)  
**Exaltation:** Libra  
**Detriment:** Cancer and Leo  
**Fall:** Aries  

## Significance
Saturn represents discipline, structure, responsibility, limitations, time, and maturity. It is the taskmaster planet, bringing lessons through challenges and demanding hard work and accountability. Saturn governs authority, boundaries, career structures, and life lessons.

## In the Birth Chart
Saturn's sign shows where you face challenges, develop discipline, and build mastery. The house placement reveals the area of life requiring the most work and responsibility. Saturn spends about 2.5 years in each sign.

## Transits
Saturn transits are slow, lasting about 2.5 years per sign, and bring tests, restructuring, and maturation. Saturn return (every 29.5 years) is one of the most significant astrological events, marking major life transitions and the call to step into greater responsibility and authenticity.
