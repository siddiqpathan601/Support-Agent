# The Sun ☉

**Rules:** Leo  
**Exaltation:** Aries  
**Detriment:** Aquarius  
**Fall:** Libra  

## Significance
The Sun represents the core self, ego, vitality, and life purpose. It is the most important placement in a natal chart, indicating one's fundamental identity, willpower, and the qualities one is developing in this lifetime. The Sun sign reveals how a person shines, what motivates them, and their essential creative expression.

## In the Birth Chart
The Sun's sign shows your core personality and approach to life. The house placement shows the area of life where you seek to express your identity most fully. The Sun takes approximately one month to move through each sign and one year to complete the zodiac.

## Transits
When transiting planets aspect your natal Sun, they activate your sense of self, vitality, and life direction. Sun transits are felt strongly and personally, affecting confidence, health, and overall energy levels.

# The Moon ☽

**Rules:** Cancer  
**Exaltation:** Taurus  
**Detriment:** Capricorn  
**Fall:** Scorpio  

## Significance
The Moon represents emotions, instincts, habits, the subconscious, and the inner world. It governs how you process feelings, what makes you feel safe, and your emotional needs. The Moon also connects to the mother, nurturing, and early childhood conditioning.

## In the Birth Chart
The Moon sign shows your emotional nature and instinctive reactions. The house placement reveals where you seek emotional security and comfort. The Moon moves quickly, changing signs every 2.5 days, making the exact birth time important for Moon sign accuracy.

## Transits
Lunar transits are fast-moving and affect daily moods and emotional responses. Monthly New and Full Moons activate different areas of the chart, creating cycles of emotional beginnings and culminations.

# Mercury ☿

**Rules:** Gemini and Virgo  
**Exaltation:** Virgo  
**Detriment:** Sagittarius and Pisces  
**Fall:** Pisces  

## Significance
Mercury represents communication, intellect, reasoning, learning, and the processing of information. It governs how you think, speak, write, and make connections. Mercury also rules short-distance travel, siblings, and the nervous system.

## In the Birth Chart
Mercury's sign shows your communication style and thinking patterns. Its house placement indicates the area of life where you focus your mental energy. Mercury retrograde periods (3-4 times per year) are associated with communication disruptions, travel delays, and the need to review and revise.

## Transits
Mercury transits are short-lived (about 2 days per sign) but affect daily communication, decisions, and mental clarity. Mercury retrograde periods last about 3 weeks and call for reflection rather than initiating new plans.
