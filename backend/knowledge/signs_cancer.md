# Cancer ♋ (June 21 – July 22)

**Element:** Water  
**Modality:** Cardinal  
**Ruling Planet:** Moon  

## Core Traits
Cancer is the nurturer of the zodiac, deeply connected to emotions, home, and family. Cancer natives are intuitive, protective, and emotionally intelligent. They create safety and belonging wherever they go.

## Strengths
- Deep emotional intelligence and empathy
- Strong intuition and psychic sensitivity
- Devoted to family and loved ones
- Creative and imaginative
- Tenacious and protective

## Challenges
- Moodiness and emotional sensitivity
- Difficulty letting go of the past
- Tendency to be over-protective or clingy
- Can retreat into a shell when hurt
- Indirect communication when upset

## In Relationships
Cancer partners are deeply nurturing, loyal, and emotionally available. They create warm, secure home environments and express love through caregiving, cooking, and emotional support. They seek deep emotional bonds.

## Career Tendencies
Cancer excels in caregiving, nursing, counseling, social work, hospitality, real estate, cooking, childcare, and any role involving nurturing or creating safe spaces.
