# Gemini ♊ (May 21 – June 20)

**Element:** Air  
**Modality:** Mutable  
**Ruling Planet:** Mercury  

## Core Traits
Gemini is the communicator and intellectual explorer of the zodiac. Gemini natives are curious, adaptable, and thrive on mental stimulation and social exchange. They are the storytellers, connectors, and information gatherers of the zodiac.

## Strengths
- Excellent communication and wit
- Highly adaptable and versatile
- Intellectually curious and quick-thinking
- Socially engaging and charming
- Ability to see multiple perspectives

## Challenges
- Inconsistency and scattered focus
- Superficiality or avoidance of emotional depth
- Restlessness and difficulty with commitment
- Tendency to overthink or become anxious

## In Relationships
Gemini partners need intellectual stimulation and variety. They express love through conversation, humor, and shared experiences. They thrive with partners who respect their need for freedom and mental engagement.

## Career Tendencies
Gemini excels in journalism, writing, teaching, sales, marketing, public relations, technology, translation, and any role involving communication or multiple projects.
