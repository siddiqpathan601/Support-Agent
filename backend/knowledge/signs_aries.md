# Aries ♈ (March 21 – April 19)

**Element:** Fire  
**Modality:** Cardinal  
**Ruling Planet:** Mars  

## Core Traits
Aries is the first sign of the zodiac, embodying raw initiative, courage, and the spark of new beginnings. Aries natives are natural leaders driven by passion, independence, and an unshakable desire to pioneer. They approach life headfirst with enthusiasm and directness.

## Strengths
- Courageous and willing to take risks
- High energy and physical vitality
- Natural leadership and initiative
- Honest and direct communication
- Optimistic and action-oriented

## Challenges
- Impatience and impulsiveness
- Tendency toward short-lived interests
- Can be confrontational or aggressive when frustrated
- Difficulty with long-term planning and patience

## In Relationships
Aries partners are passionate, loyal, and protective. They need independence within relationships and thrive with partners who match their energy. They show love through action rather than words.

## Career Tendencies
Aries excels in roles requiring leadership, quick decision-making, entrepreneurship, athletics, emergency services, and any field demanding courage and initiative.
